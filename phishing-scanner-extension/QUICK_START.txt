================================================================================
  PHISHING EMAIL SCANNER - QUICK START GUIDE
================================================================================

✅ STEP 1: Icons Created!
   The icon files have been automatically generated.

✅ STEP 2: Load Extension in Chrome/Edge
   
   FOR CHROME:
   1. Open Chrome browser
   2. Type in address bar: chrome://extensions/
   3. Toggle ON "Developer mode" (top-right corner)
   4. Click "Load unpacked" button
   5. Navigate to: E:\python\phishing-scanner-extension
   6. Click "Select Folder"
   
   FOR EDGE:
   1. Open Microsoft Edge browser
   2. Type in address bar: edge://extensions/
   3. Toggle ON "Developer mode" (bottom-left)
   4. Click "Load unpacked" button
   5. Navigate to: E:\python\phishing-scanner-extension
   6. Click "Select Folder"

✅ STEP 3: Verify Installation
   - You should see "Phishing Email Scanner" in your extensions list
   - Extension icon should appear in browser toolbar (shield icon)

✅ STEP 4: Test the Extension
   
   1. Go to Gmail:
      - Open: https://mail.google.com
      - Sign in to your account
   
   2. OR go to Outlook:
      - Open: https://outlook.live.com
      - Sign in to your account
   
   3. Open any email:
      - Click on an email to open it
      - The extension automatically scans it
   
   4. Look for warnings:
      - RED banner = High Risk phishing detected
      - ORANGE banner = Medium Risk phishing detected
      - No banner = Email appears safe

✅ STEP 5: Test Extension Popup
   - Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar
   - You should see a popup showing scanner status
   - You can enable/disable the scanner from here

================================================================================
  TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Problem: Extension won't load
Solution: 
  - Make sure all files are in E:\python\phishing-scanner-extension
  - Check that manifest.json exists
  - Verify icons folder has icon48.png and icon128.png

Problem: No warnings showing
Solution:
  - Make sure you're on Gmail or Outlook web interface (not mobile app)
  - Try refreshing the page (F5)
  - Open an actual email (not just inbox view)
  - Check browser console (F12) for errors

Problem: Extension icon not visible
Solution:
  - Click the puzzle piece icon (extensions menu) in Chrome/Edge
  - Pin "Phishing Email Scanner" to toolbar
